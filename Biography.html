Childhood


           Back when I was a child I used to play all the time outside with my cousins. We like to play the filipino games called tumbang preso, luksung baka and many more. I always go out at the afternoon to play and go home at night. I only took kinder 1 and kinder 2 in preschool in our school called D’Paul Kiddie Center Located at Malinao Pasig City. After that I got straight to grade 1 in which I took a test and I passed in the test. I gained many new friends in gradeschool. I studied at Pasig Catholic College up until grade 6 in which I gained new friends to interact with. It was a rollercoaster journey for me because it was not easy studying as I always fail in the mathematics subject. I always have pocket food when I was studying and my food was always rice and three pieces of Jumbo hotdog by Tender Juicy. Sometime when I’m tired of eating just hotdog, I buy french fries downstairs at the canteen So that I can eat the hotdog with a twist. I also love to buy Real Leaf Iced Tea because it is a very sweet ice tea. It sometimes has an event like starbucks where you gather coupons for you to exchange free items like pins, planners, cap, pencil case and more. The real leaf iced tea was a product of the coca cola company thats why all of the free items have a coca cola sign. I gained that time three planners and I gave the extra ones to my childhood friends. When it was vacation time it was traditional for my fathers friends that we and them have an outing outside of town like places in Laguna. We always go there to have swimming vacation and it was so much fun swimming with my childhood friends. Since grade 1 we always have this dance called ground demo every foundation week which was in february.We always have a ground demonstration from all the levels in school from preschool to college level and each grade have their own unique category. I experienced dancing in front of many people and at first it was very nervous because Im not the of person who can dance in front of many people. I’m a shy person but as I grow older I get used to it because every year we dance in front of our parents and friends and it takes a long time of preparation for our dance.

College


           I am Zach Emmañuelle Valdez Martin and I live in Palatiw, Pasig City located at Metro Manila. I stopped a year because for me online class was not for me and it was hard to learn in an online setup. During the time that I stopped I worked at the business of our family. It was a water refilling station in which I washed the containers and fill them up with water after washing. I started working at 8am and ended the shift at 5pm. It was nice working because after working I get my salary for the day. Having the salary that I worked for is fun because I can buy/eat anything I want. A year passed by and I worked on my papers to get into the University of the East. At first it was hard because in college you meet new people around you. At first it was hard because I'm a shy person but then later on I was getting used to it. I was not shy anymore with my classmates because as time passed by we get to know each other. Having a group activity is an example of getting to know each other and we can also see the weaknesses of each other. We help each other surpass our limits because we don't want anyone to fail in our class. It was a bumpy ride because at first we don't have any backgrounds towards programming well some of us have already. We did our best and we passed our subjects and we moved on towards our second year in college. Our second year 1st semester was still online because it was not still hard to act or go to class. It was easy because we dont have to wake up early to travel to school. Some of us lived far from the school thats why we tend to leave early to avoid the traffic. Our second semester is Face to face thats why its hard for us because we can't get enough rest after school. It is a nice experience because finally I can experience going to school in college because I started my college life in an online setup that's why it was not that good experience for me. I also joined the UE Pep Squad because I miss being an athlete and I just wanna try again being an athlete.
